## Why This Works for Consistency

This structure forces models to:

1. **Lock the environment as a single canonical location**
2. **Generate multiple camera angles instead of new scenes**
3. **Preserve geometry and terrain**
4. **Create a reusable “environment bible” for later scenes**

This is the same technique used in **film environment scouting sheets**.
